2. Data We Collect

Account data: Email address, username, display name, profile picture (via Apple Sign-In, Google Sign-In, or email magic link).

Physiological data (with explicit consent): Weight, height, date of birth, gender. Used for training metrics and matchmaking.

Location data (with consent): GPS coordinates collected only during active runs. Raw coordinates are processed server-side for distance calculation and immediately discarded. Other users see only your relative distance, never your location.

Fitness data: Run distance, duration, pace, estimated calories, step count, cadence, VDOT score, fatigue profile.

Competitive data: ELO rating, match history, rank, XP, coins, power usage, season pass progression.

Social data: Friend connections, feed posts, likes, comments, club membership, club chat messages.

Device data: Device model, OS version, mock-location flag (for anti-cheat).

Payment data: Transaction receipts and subscription status from Apple/Google. We never receive payment-card details.

Downloaded content: Coach+ automatically downloads an AI voice model (~80 MB) on first activation. Music+ downloads audio tracks for offline playback. Stored locally on your device; may use mobile data.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Delivering the core service (run tracking, matchmaking, ELO).
  • Anti-cheat and match-integrity validation.
  • AI Coach analysis and training recommendations.
  • Push notifications you have opted into.

4. AI-Powered Features (EU AI Act Compliance)

AI Coach. Generates personalized coaching text using OpenAI. Your run data and profile are sent to OpenAI under a Data Processing Agreement. OpenAI does not use your data to train its models. Text-to-speech is processed entirely on your device. You must consent before first use. Limited risk category.

Matchmaking. ELO-based algorithm matching players by competitive performance only. No personal characteristics used. Minimal risk.

Anti-cheat. Automated GPS validation detecting impossible movement patterns. May invalidate match results. You may request human review. Minimal risk.

Fatigue analysis. Runs entirely on your device using local data. Minimal risk.

AI-generated music. Music tracks available through Music+ are AI-generated compositions created using machine-learning models. They are not performed or composed by human artists.

We do not use AI for biometric identification, social scoring, emotion recognition, or any prohibited purpose under the EU AI Act.

6. Strava Integration

Connecting your Strava account is optional. If you choose to connect, AURENA acts as an independent data controller for the data we receive from Strava, separate from Strava itself. The two scopes we request are:

  • activity:read_all — used once per Strava connection to import summaries (Strava activity id, distance, duration, pace, cadence, elevation gain, start time) of your recent running activities, used to calculate your starting ELO. We use the last 90 days; if that window contains fewer than three runs we extend the search to the last 180 days. Only activities of type Run, Trail Run, or Virtual Run are read — never cycling, walking, swimming, or any other sport. We do not import GPS coordinates, photos, comments, or kudos. After the seed, we never call this scope again unless you disconnect and reconnect (which is rate-limited to once every 30 days).
  • activity:write — used only when you explicitly tap "Share to Strava" on a finished AURENA run, to upload that one run as a Strava activity on your behalf. We never upload silently and we never schedule background uploads.

Cached Strava activity summaries are deleted automatically seven (7) days after import, in line with the Strava API Agreement. We do not use Strava data to train, fine-tune, or evaluate any AI or machine-learning model — neither AI Coach nor any other feature receives Strava data. We do not display Strava data belonging to one user to any other user; only the derived ELO seed is visible on leaderboards.

Your Strava OAuth tokens are encrypted at rest using AES-256-GCM and only used by our server to talk to Strava on your behalf. When you disconnect (Settings → Strava → Disconnect) or delete your AURENA account, we revoke our access at Strava and delete every cached Strava-derived row from our database. You can revoke our access independently at any time from your Strava settings page on strava.com.

The Strava privacy policy applies to the data Strava continues to hold about you and is available at strava.com/legal/privacy.

8. Data Retention

  • Account data, runs, posts, matches: until you delete your account.
  • Raw GPS: processed in real time and immediately discarded.
  • Club chat messages: automatically deleted after 30 days.
  • ELO audit log: 2 years.
  • Coach memory: rolling window of last 10 runs.
  • Strava cache (activity summaries): maximum 7 days from import.

Account deletion is immediate and permanent.
