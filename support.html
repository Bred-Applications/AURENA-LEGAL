Strava Connection Issues

If your Strava connection is not working as expected, please first try:

  • Open the app → Profile → Settings (gear icon) → tap Disconnect on the Strava row, then reconnect.
  • Make sure your Strava account has at least three running activities in the last 90 days (we extend to 180 days if you have fewer).
  • Confirm you authorized both view activities and upload activities when redirected to Strava.

Still stuck? Email us at the address above with a short description and your AURENA username and we will look into it.

Privacy and Data Requests

To exercise your rights under GDPR (access, rectification, erasure, portability, objection), use the app's settings — Profile → Settings → Download My Data for a full export, or email jb@bredapplications.com for any other request. See the Privacy Policy for full details, including our Strava integration policy.

Account Deletion

You can delete your AURENA account at any time from Profile → Settings → Delete Account. Deletion is immediate and permanent and includes:

  • Revoking AURENA's access at Strava on your behalf via Strava's deauthorize endpoint.
  • Purging every cached Strava activity summary, upload record, and OAuth token from our database.
  • Removing your AURENA profile, runs, posts, friendships, club memberships, and all derived statistics.

You may also revoke AURENA's Strava access independently from your Strava settings page.
